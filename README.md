# Business Sales Performance Analytics

Interactive Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard using Power BI.

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore Sales dataset. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights by analyzing sales trends, product performance, category performance, and regional performance.

## Objective

To analyze business sales data and provide actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel

## Dataset

- Sample Superstore Sales Dataset
- Records: 9,995
- Columns: 21

## Dashboard Highlights

- Sales, Profit, Orders, and Quantity KPIs
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales by Category
- Top 10 Best-Selling Products
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Region
- Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Segment, Order Date)

## Key Insights

- Technology is the highest-performing product category by sales.
- The West region generates the highest sales and profit.
- A small group of products contributes significantly to overall revenue.
- Sales vary across different time periods, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
- Interactive filters enable detailed analysis across multiple business dimensions.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-performing product categories.
- Replicate successful strategies from top-performing regions.
- Strengthen sales initiatives in lower-performing regions.
- Monitor sales performance regularly using interactive dashboards.
